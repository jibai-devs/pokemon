## Win condition (summary)

1. Set up 2–3 Dragapult ex with energy to chain **Phantom Dive** ({R}{P}, 200 dmg to Active + 6 damage counters spread anywhere on opponent's Bench).
2. Convert bench spread into knockouts via **Munkidori's Adrena-Brain** (move up to 3 damage counters from your own Pokémon onto an opponent's Pokémon).
3. Use **Boss's Orders** + prize-mapping to pick off the right targets to reach 6 prizes first.

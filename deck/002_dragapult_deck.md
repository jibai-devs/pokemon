# 002 Dragapult Deck ("Pult Noir")

Dragapult ex Phantom Dive engine — see `dragapult_deck_explanation.md`
(repo root) for full strategy, matchup notes, and card-by-card rationale.

## Win condition

Set up 2-3 Dragapult ex with enough energy attachments to chain **Phantom
Dive** ({R}{P}, 200 dmg + 6 damage counters spread across the opponent's
bench). Combine the bench spread with **Munkidori's Adrena-Brain** ability
(move up to 3 damage counters between Pokémon) to convert near-KOs into
real ones, then use **Boss's Orders** and prize-mapping to close the game.

## Summary

Model a probability distribution over what the opponent's deck/hand likely
contains, using archetype clusters and card-frequency data from the meta
analysis (PKM-012), and update that belief as the opponent reveals cards
during a game. Feed sampled "determinizations" (concrete guesses at the
opponent's full deck consistent with current beliefs) into the MCTS search
from PKM-014 — this is Information Set MCTS (ISMCTS), the standard technique
for search under hidden information in card games (used by poker bots,
Hearthstone/MTG bots, etc.).

## Motivation

CABT is a POMDP, not an MDP — opponent's hand is hidden at inference, so
plain MCTS (which assumes full state visibility) doesn't directly apply.
Rather than search blind, use what we already know about the meta (from
PKM-012's archetype clusters and per-archetype card frequencies) as a prior,
and narrow it down as the opponent's revealed cards rule out inconsistent
archetypes.

## Approach

1. **Prior**: initialize belief over archetypes using their meta prevalence
   from PKM-012's output (`data/meta_report.txt`), not a worst-case
   assumption — worst-case/maximin priors are for robustness against an
   adversary who knows your exploit, not for expected-value play against the
   real field distribution
2. **Update**: each time the opponent plays/reveals a card, Bayesian-update
   archetype probabilities using per-archetype card frequency (archetypes
   that commonly run that card gain mass, others lose it)
3. **Determinize**: at decision time, sample one or more full opponent
   decklists/hands from the current posterior, consistent with cards already
   observed
4. **Search**: run PKM-014's MCTS against the sampled determinization(s),
   averaging across multiple samples per real decision to approximate
   reasoning under uncertainty

## Success criterion

ISMCTS-equipped agent beats plain MCTS-without-opponent-modeling (PKM-014) in
head-to-head evaluation, particularly in games that go long enough for
several opponent cards to be revealed.

## Notes

- Hard dependency on PKM-012 — need real archetype clusters + per-archetype

- Also depends on PKM-014 (MCTS search) existing as the consumer of sampled
  determinizations
- Treat as an enhancement layered onto the BC/PPO + MCTS pipeline, not a
  blocker for the Aug 9 simulation entry deadline

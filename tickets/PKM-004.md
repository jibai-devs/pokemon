## Summary

`score_option` in `agent.py` and `format_option` in `catalog.py` have `OptionType` 7 and 8 swapped relative to the engine's real enum. This means the agent is scoring energy attachment as trainer plays, and trainer plays as energy attachment — corrupting two of the most common action types.

**Real engine (confirmed from official API docs):**
- `7 = PLAY` — play a card from hand (field `index`)
- `8 = ATTACH` — attach energy/tool (fields `area, index, inPlayArea, inPlayIndex`)

**Current code (wrong):**
- `opt_type == 7` → scored as energy attachment
- `opt_type == 8` → scored as trainer/item play

## Acceptance criteria

- `score_option` branches on `opt_type == 7` for playing cards and `opt_type == 8` for attaching
- `format_option` labels `7` as `PLAY` and `8` as `ATTACH`
- A verbose game log shows no `?type=N` entries for types 7 and 8
- `uv run pokemon-play -v -g 1` completes without errors

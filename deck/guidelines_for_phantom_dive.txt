PHANTOM DIVE THIS TURN? (attack-or-hold-back decision tree)

Goal: decide whether to attack with Phantom Dive at the end of this turn, or
stay in setup phase (hold back / keep developing instead).

1. Can I Phantom Dive at the end of this turn?
   - No  -> STAY IN SETUP PHASE
   - Yes -> go to 2

2. If I PD, will I win the game?
   - Yes -> AIM TO PHANTOM DIVE AT END OF TURN
   - No  -> go to 3

3. If I PD, will the Dragapult I attached energy to (the one attacking)
   die (e.g. get knocked out on the opponent's following turn)?
   - No  -> AIM TO PHANTOM DIVE AT END OF TURN
   - Yes -> go to 4

4. Will I still be able to Phantom Dive next turn even after this Dragapult
   dies? For example, if I...
     a. have a Drakloak with 1 energy on bench + can find another
        Dragapult ex + 1 more energy, or
     b. have a Drakloak + can find and use Crispin (e.g. via Meowth ex) +
        Psychic and Fire energy present in deck
   - Yes -> AIM TO PHANTOM DIVE AT END OF TURN
   - No  -> STAY IN SETUP PHASE

Terminal outcomes: "aim to Phantom Dive at end of turn" or
"stay in setup phase" -- i.e. attack now if it's lethal, or if attacking
doesn't actually cost you your attacker for nothing (it wasn't surviving
anyway, or you can rebuild the attack next turn even after this Dragapult
dies); otherwise hold back and keep developing instead.

## Resolution

Built `scripts/build_deck_reference.py --deck <name>` (reusable across
decks, reads from `pokemon.decks.DECKS`) which pulls HP/type/weakness
/resistance/retreat, ability text, and attack cost/damage/text from
`all_cards.json` + `all_attacks.json`, fixing the mojibake in the raw
catalog text. Generated `deck/001_psychic_deck_reference.md` for all 24
unique cards in `PSYCHIC_DECK`.

Reading the reference sheet card-by-card surfaced the deck's actual win
condition (per the user's teammate): **Slowking's Seek Inspiration**
discards the top deck card and, if it's a Pokemon without a Rule Box
(i.e. not ex/Mega ex/V), copies one of its attacks for free. Cross-
referencing which Pokemon in this deck lack Rule Boxes (Slowpoke,
Slowking, Metagross, Kyurem, Zeraora — every other Pokemon in the list is
ex) shows Metagross and Kyurem are deliberate high-damage discard fodder,
not dead cards as I'd assumed in the prior turn — Metagross's missing
evolution line and Kyurem's uncastable energy cost are irrelevant since
Slowking never pays them directly. Ciphermaniac's Codebreaking and
Academy at Night turn the "random" top-of-deck discard into a controlled
one. Full writeup in `deck/001_psychic_deck.md`'s Strategy section.

## Summary

Pull exact stats for all 24 unique card IDs in `pokemon.decks.PSYCHIC_DECK`
from `reverse-engineering/data/all_cards.json`, `all_attacks.json`, and
`data/cards_processed.csv` — HP, attack costs/damage, abilities, retreat
cost, weakness/resistance — into a single reference doc/table.

## Motivation

General Pokemon-TCG knowledge isn't reliable for this specific card pool
(exact numbers for newer cards like Mega Kangaskhan ex, Ciphermaniac's
Codebreaking aren't confidently known). The heuristic agent (PKM-017)
needs grounded, correct numbers rather than guessed ones, and this
doubles as source material for the Strategy report's deck-score writeup
(20% of scoring).

## Steps

1. For each of the 24 unique IDs in `PSYCHIC_DECK`, look up full card
   record (`all_cards.json`) and any attached attacks (`all_attacks.json`,
   via attack IDs on the card record)
2. Cross-reference `data/cards_processed.csv` for HP/retreat/weakness
   columns already normalized by PKM-013
3. Note any abilities (not just attacks) — e.g. on Slowking, Metagross —
   since ability text isn't in the attack catalog
4. Write up as a table (card, HP, type, attack name/cost/damage,
   ability text, retreat cost, weakness) — likely as
   `deck/001_psychic_deck_reference.md` alongside the existing decklist doc

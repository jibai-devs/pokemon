## Summary

Fine-tune the BC policy via PPO self-play to push beyond imitation and discover stronger lines.

## Approach

- Start from BC checkpoint (PKM-009) — do not train from scratch
- Self-play: current policy vs a frozen snapshot of itself (update snapshot every N steps)
- Reward: +1 win, -1 loss, 0 draw (terminal reward only, no shaping initially)
- PPO clip: ε=0.2, value loss coeff=0.5, entropy bonus=0.01
- Run via WSL (libcg.so required for game simulation)

## Success criterion

PPO policy wins > 60% head-to-head against the BC-only checkpoint over 200 games.

## Notes

- Do not start RL until BC policy already beats random by > 70% — starting RL from a weak policy is unstable
- The value head trained here is the critic; re-initialise it at RL start even if BC partially trained it
- Keep entropy bonus to prevent premature collapse to a single strategy
- Gate on PKM-009 being complete and validated first
